FlöFuel Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 18, 2026

Overview

FlöFuel is a cycling nutrition app for iPhone and Apple Watch. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data FlöFuel accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

FlöFuel does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. There are no FlöFuel servers. All data stays on your device.

Health Data (HealthKit)

FlöFuel can optionally read your body weight from Apple HealthKit to calculate personalized hydration targets. This is entirely optional — you can enter your weight manually instead. FlöFuel never writes data to HealthKit. Weight data is used only for on-device calculations and is never transmitted off your device.

Location Data

FlöFuel requests location access at city-level accuracy only (kCLLocationAccuracyKilometer) to detect local weather conditions for hydration adjustments. Your location is used ephemerally — it is not stored, logged, or transmitted to FlöFuel servers (there are none).

Weather Services

FlöFuel uses two weather services to determine temperature for hydration calculations:

  • Apple WeatherKit — an Apple system service. Data is handled according to Apple's privacy policy.
  • Open-Meteo API — a fallback weather service. Your approximate latitude and longitude are sent to api.open-meteo.com to retrieve current temperature. Open-Meteo is a public API that requires no authentication, no user account, and no user identifier. See Open-Meteo's terms for details.

Session Data

Ride timestamps, fuel intake events, and GI comfort ratings are stored locally on your device using SwiftData (SQLite). This data never leaves your device.

Apple Watch & WatchConnectivity

FlöFuel syncs programs and session state between iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework and App Groups. All communication is local, on-device only.

No Analytics, Tracking, or Ads

FlöFuel does not include any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking tools of any kind. We do not track your usage of the app.

No Accounts

FlöFuel does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since FlöFuel does not collect any personal data, there is nothing to share with third parties.

Children's Privacy

FlöFuel does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

All FlöFuel data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling FlöFuel completely deletes all app data. There is no server-side data to request deletion of.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
