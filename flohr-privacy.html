FlöHR Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 1, 2026

Overview

FlöHR is a simple heart rate monitoring app for Apple Watch and iPhone. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data FlöHR accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

FlöHR does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data stays on your device.

Health Data

FlöHR reads heart rate data from Apple HealthKit to display your real-time heart rate during workout sessions. This data is accessed only with your explicit permission and is never sent off your device. FlöHR may also write workout and heart rate data back to HealthKit so it appears in the Apple Health app.

No Accounts

FlöHR does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

No Analytics or Tracking

FlöHR does not include any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track your usage of the app.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since FlöHR does not collect any data, there is nothing to share with third parties.

Children's Privacy

FlöHR does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version at this URL. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at github.com/ebowman/privacy.
